Accessibility Statement
NetPariksha.com is committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability. We strive to ensure that our digital services are inclusive and usable for everyone, including people with disabilities.
Our Commitment
  • We follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA as our accessibility standard.
  • We design and test our website to be compatible with screen readers, keyboard navigation, and assistive technologies.
  • We use high-contrast text, logical heading structure, descriptive links, and alt text for images.
  • We aim to ensure all interactive elements are accessible by keyboard and touch.
  • We continually review and improve accessibility features as technology and standards evolve.
Accessibility Principles (POUR)
  • Perceivable: Information and user interface components are presented in ways users can perceive (e.g., alt text, contrast, captions).
  • Operable: All functionality is available from a keyboard and accessible for users with different input methods.
  • Understandable: Content and navigation are clear, predictable, and easy to follow.
  • Robust: Content is compatible with current and future assistive technologies.
Continuous Improvement

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We regularly audit and update our site to improve usability for all. We recommend using the latest browsers and assistive technologies for the best experience.
