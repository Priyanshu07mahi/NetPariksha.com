Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have important rights regarding their personal information. NetPariksha.com is committed to transparency and respecting your privacy choices.
Your CCPA Rights
  • Right to Know: You have the right to request that we disclose what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal information that we hold.
  • Right to Access: You have the right to request a copy of the specific personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You have the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: You have the right not to be discriminated against for exercising any of your privacy rights.
NetPariksha.com Does Not Sell Your Personal Information
